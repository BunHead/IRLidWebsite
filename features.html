How it works

<

The "In Real Life ID" app revolutionizes the way we verify real-world encounters, bridging the gap between digital authentication and human interaction. At its core, the app allows two individuals to meet in person and use their mobile devices to scan each other's unique QR codes. This simple yet powerful action triggers the generation of a third, distinctive QR code, sent to both participants as a digital acknowledgment of their meeting. What sets this process apart is not just the mutual verification of personhood but the incorporation of precise geographical data along with a date and time stamp. This ensures that the encounter is not only verified by both parties involved but is also recorded with exact spatial and temporal details, providing a robust proof of meeting. This feature is especially valuable in contexts requiring trusted verification of in-person interactions, from social meetings to secure transactions, offering peace of mind through a verifiable digital footprint of real-world connections.
